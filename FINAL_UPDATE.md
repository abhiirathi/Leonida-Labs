## 🔧 Changes Made

### 1. **Wallpaper Display Mode**
Changed from `object-fit: cover` to `object-fit: contain`

**Result**: The entire wallpaper is now visible, including:
- ✅ "Grand Theft Auto" logo at the top
- ✅ "VI" logo at the bottom center
- ✅ Rockstar Games logo at the bottom right
- ✅ Jason and Lucia (the main characters)
- ✅ Complete Vice City skyline background

### 2. **Background Enhancement**
Added a beautiful gradient background behind the wallpaper:
```css
background: linear-gradient(135deg, 
  #1a0a2e 0%,    /* Deep purple */
  #0f0520 25%,   /* Dark purple */
  #16213e 50%,   /* Navy blue */
  #0f0520 75%,   /* Dark purple */
  #1a0a2e 100%   /* Deep purple */
);
```

This creates a stunning GTA 6-themed backdrop that complements the wallpaper!

### 3. **Vignette Optimization**
Reduced vignette opacity to ensure logos are clearly visible:
- Bottom vignette: `rgba(5, 5, 7, 0.08)` (was 0.18)
- Maintains text readability while showing the full image

### 4. **Mobile Optimization**
Updated responsive breakpoints to show the full wallpaper on all devices:
- **Desktop**: Full wallpaper with gradient background
- **Tablet**: Full wallpaper maintained
- **Mobile**: Full wallpaper with optimized vignette
